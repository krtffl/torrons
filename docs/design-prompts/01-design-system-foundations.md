# Prompt 01 — Design system foundations

**Set:** A (existing site, redesigned)
**Status:** Foundational — applies to every screen below. Read/commission this one first; everything else assumes its palette, type scale, and component styles are locked.

## Prompt (copy-paste this into Claude Design)

```
Design a visual identity for "Torrorèndum," a Catalan-language head-to-head voting competition where people compare pairs of torrons (Catalan Christmas nougat) from the heritage brand Torrons Vicens (founded 1775, Agramunt, PGI-protected craft). Ground the palette in warm browns, caramel/gold, and cream — NOT generic Christmas red-and-green, no snowflakes or holly. Keep a deep burgundy as a single "competition" accent color reserved for results, winners, and calls to action. Typography should pair a confident, heavy display sans for headlines and UI labels with a warm, readable serif for longer text — the tone is "museum-grade craft heritage" crossed with "playful, internet-native flavor drops" (the brand does both 1775-heritage marketing and viral flavors like Dubai chocolate). Design for mobile-first, and make every key screen look intentional as a screenshot — this will be shared on Instagram and WhatsApp constantly. Also reserve a quiet, unused placeholder slot in the header/footer for a future "amb la col·laboració de Torrons Vicens" logo lockup, styled but empty for now. Deliver: color tokens, type scale, button/card styles, iconography direction, and a light + dark treatment.
```
