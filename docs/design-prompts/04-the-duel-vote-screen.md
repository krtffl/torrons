## Prompt (copy-paste this into Claude Design)

```
Torrorèndum: Catalan voting competition for Torrons Vicens, warm brown/gold/cream palette, burgundy accent, heavy sans + warm serif type. Design the core voting screen used throughout the open-season phase: two torrons shown side by side, tap one to vote. Make this feel like a real round, not a form — a large typographic "VS" between the cards, satisfying tap/hover states, a progress indicator toward unlocking results, and a quiet sense of momentum (streak count or vote count nearby). Cards show product photo, name, and light quick-facts (intensity icon, allergen icons, "new this year" badge) without cluttering the decision. Design the post-vote transition as one smooth beat achievable with CSS transitions and HTMX partial swaps — no heavy client-side animation framework.
```
